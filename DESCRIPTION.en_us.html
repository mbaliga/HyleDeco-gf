Hyle Deco is a hairline display typeface drawn for interfaces where a single thin line of light on a black screen carries the identity. It is built on one constant stroke of 44 units on a 1000 unit em, with a 700 unit cap height and a 500 unit x height. The letterforms are geometric and deco in spirit, with flat terminals, rounded rectangular bowls, and numerals treated as a first class channel with tabular spacing available. The family ships an upright and its italic, drawn from hand made vector letterforms by Madhav.

Hyle Deco is developed at github.com/mbaliga/hyledeco-gf.
